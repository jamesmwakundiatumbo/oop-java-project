FOR MCAs, WARD ADMINS & OFFICIALS — USING CIVICTRACK

If you are a Member of County Assembly (MCA), a ward administrator, or a public works / service official, CivicTrack helps you see what residents report and manage assignments in one place.

FOR OFFICIALS
• Sign in with an Official account.
• View issues assigned to you by an administrator (or ward workflow as configured).
• Update status as work progresses: PENDING → IN_PROGRESS → RESOLVED.
• Read and respond via comments where the app supports it.

FOR ADMINISTRATORS (WARD / SYSTEM)
• See all reported issues.
• Assign issues to the appropriate official.
• Use reports and statistics (where implemented) to view workload by status and priority.
